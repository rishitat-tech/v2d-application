EGO Setup & Gesture Guide

This guide explains the starting and ending gesture requirements and what should stay visible in the head-mounted camera view.

Camera View Requirements

  • The camera is head-mounted and should capture the task from the collector's point of view.
  • Fingers and wrists should stay fully visible.
  • Hands should not be covered by clothes.
  • No other person or extra hand should be visible inside the head-mounted camera view.
  • The task interaction should remain clear and in frame.

Initial and End Hand Gesture

At the start and end of each session, hold the gesture with palms facing each other and fingers bent.

  • Hold the gesture steadily for 3 seconds.
  • Make sure wrists are visible in the camera field of view.
  • Keep fingers visible and not covered by clothing.
  • Use the same gesture at both the start and end of the session.

Before Starting the Task

  • Confirm hands and wrists are visible.
  • Confirm no extra hands or people are visible.
  • Hold the start gesture for 3 seconds.
  • Begin the task only after the start gesture is complete.

Quick Checklist

  • Head-mounted camera view is clear.
  • Wrists are visible.
  • Fingers are visible.
  • No extra hands or people are in view.
  • Start gesture held for 3 seconds.
  • End gesture held for 3 seconds.

Visibility is important. If hands, wrists, or the task interaction leave the frame, the recording may not be usable.
