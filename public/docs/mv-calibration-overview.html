MV Calibration

Calibration Overview

This overview explains why calibration is needed before MV data collection. For full steps, use the MV Calibration Guide.

Why Calibration Is Used

Calibration helps align the camera views and improves the quality of MV data collection. It supports extrinsic camera pose calibration, which helps the system understand where each camera is positioned in 3D space.

What Calibration Uses

  • A calibration board that meets the required expectations.
  • Secure cameras that are not moving.
  • A clear marked bounding area.
  • Slow and smooth board movement to reduce motion blur.

Before Data Collection

After calibration is complete, the calibration video should be checked for quality before starting data collection. This increases the chance of collecting clean and high-quality data.
