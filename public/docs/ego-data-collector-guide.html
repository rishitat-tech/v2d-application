EGO Data Collector Guide

This guide explains what the data collector should do during EGO recording.

Start and End Pose

  • Start each session with palms facing each other and fingers bent.
  • Hold the pose steadily for 3 seconds.
  • Return to the same pose at the end of the session.
  • No 3 second hold is required at the end.

Visibility Requirements

  • Keep fingers fully visible.
  • Keep wrists fully visible.
  • Do not cover hands or wrists with clothing.
  • Keep the task interaction inside the head-mounted camera view.

Movement Expectations

  • Move slowly, smoothly, and naturally.
  • Use deliberate and consistent grasping motions.
  • Keep speed consistent throughout the task.
  • Avoid fast, jerky, or unexpected movements.
  • Avoid unrelated pauses or unnecessary movements.
  • Avoid excessive waist rotation.
  • Do not clench the hand into a fist when not interacting with objects.

Quick Checklist

  • Start pose held for 3 seconds.
  • Hands and wrists visible.
  • Task interaction in frame.
  • Slow and natural movement.
  • No jerky movement.
  • Return to the start pose at the end.

Good EGO data should look stable, natural, and easy to understand from the head-mounted camera view.
